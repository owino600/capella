About Sawela Lodges & Capella Resort

Welcome to Sawela Lodges and Capella Resort, where luxury meets nature in perfect harmony. Nestled in the heart of Kenya's breathtaking landscapes, our properties offer an unparalleled experience of comfort and elegance.

Sawela Lodges

Experience the authentic charm of African hospitality at Sawela Lodges. Our traditional yet modern accommodations provide a perfect blend of comfort and cultural richness. Each room is thoughtfully designed to reflect the local heritage while offering contemporary amenities.

Capella Resort

Capella Resort represents the pinnacle of luxury and sophistication. With world-class facilities, gourmet dining, and exceptional service, we ensure an unforgettable stay for our guests. Our resort features stunning views, premium amenities, and exclusive experiences.
